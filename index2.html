About Ustad Hotel

Ustad Hotel in Vettuvankeni, Chennai, is a culinary destination that brings you the authentic taste of traditional recipes. Our chefs are passionate about creating dishes that honor the rich culinary heritage while adding a modern touch.

Located at 5, 2nd Ave, First Avenue, Sakthi Vinayakar Nagar, Vettuvankeni, Chennai-115, our restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy exceptional food and create lasting memories.

We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure every dish is bursting with flavor and authenticity.

Our restaurant is open from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM, serving delicious breakfast and lunch options that will delight your taste buds.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for authentic flavors, Ustad Hotel has been serving the Chennai community for years. Our name "Ustad" means master, and we strive to master the art of cooking with every dish we serve.

Our Philosophy

We believe in using traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest ingredients to create memorable dining experiences for our customers.
